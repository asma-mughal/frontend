Concept

FACT uses factoring as its PoW instead of hashing. Integer factorization solves complex mathematical puzzles related to prime numbers and theor factors. This is a different approach than just calculating hashes. This is what makes it unique. No other blockchain has so far been able to use Factoring as its POW.

Emission

There has been no premining, presale, ICO or team allocation. Thus there is no emission schedule. The only way to mint new coins is by mining. This means that supply is unlimited. The reward for factoring is proportion to the work for factoring but scaled down to avoid excessive inflation and register overflows.

Mathematics

Integer factorization is a mathematical process that involves breaking down a given integer into its prime factors, which are the fundamental building blocks of whole numbers. This process plays a crucial role in number theory and has significant importance in fields like cryptography.
